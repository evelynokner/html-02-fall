What are Tamagotchis?

Tamagotchis are a

"Tamagotchi" combinines the two Japanese words tamago (たまご) and uotchi (ウオッチ), meaning "egg" and "watch" respectively.

History of Tamagotchi

Tamagotchis first released in Japan in 1996, before releasing in the U.S. in 1997, and quickly gaining popularity. Tamagotchis were so successful that they even got banned in schools for being so distracting.
